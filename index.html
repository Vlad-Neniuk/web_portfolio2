About

Hello, I am engaged in image retouching and shooting of object and portrait photography, if necessary, and reportage, I have a small home studio for object photography and professional photographic equipment (full-frame camera Nikon Z 7 II, lenses: 24-70 mm, f/4 and Macro 105 mm, f/2.8).

I have skills in photo processing in Lightroom and Photoshop, namely: object retouching, color changing, background replacement, collage, shadow drawing, removing imperfections and unnecessary objects, color correction, and much more.

I am also learning to create infographics, vector graphics in Illustrator, and 3-D modeling in Blender. I have experience in creating photos and illustrations using artificial intelligence, namely Dall-E, and supplementing photos with generative fill in Photoshop. I always try to perform my tasks with high quality and creativity.
